This was our fifth visit to the Goucho and definitely will not be our last. Each time we visit the service is beyond reproach and it would be hard to make it any better.

In the busy downtown near a lot of other sites and amenities, this modern style restaurant is a meat lovers dream (vegetarians could come but may not feel that the price point was fair).

Food is fantastic... even if you don't eat steak... like me .... they do chicken/salmon/prawns. Great selection of salads etc. Saturday night is best night as the dancers are there and they really add to the experience.

Welcome to the Goucho restaurant

Our focus is to create a relaxing yet energetic dining experience, and our team are always eager to learn about the latest and best in Brazilian cuisine, which you can then choose from order as a starter, main or dessert. Our menu is also constantly growing, with exciting new items being prepared on site every week. In addition, we have a huge selection of soft drinks, coffee, beer and wine to complement the restaurant.
